Basil Pesto

Description

A hearty and quick pasta dish that is both easy to make and easy to eat. It can be made simply by using store-bought pesto sauce or made more complex by using homemade pesto sauce!

It can be made easily in a oven in under an hour and results in a dish that is to die for

Ingredients

  • 128gm pasta/day
  • 1 pint of cherry tomatoes/week
  • 2 1/4 cup of basil pesto/week
  • salt
  • 225gm chicken breast/day

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400F
  2. Bring a large pot of salted water to boil
  3. Cook pasta per instructions until al dente
  4. Drain
  5. Prep chicken by drying with paper towel, placing on oiled baking sheet and seasoning with salt and steak seasoning
  6. Cut tomatoes in half
  7. Use largest bowl to mix pasta, tomatoes, basil pesto and salt
  8. Prep aluminum foil boats on a baking sheet
  9. Place pasta mixture into foil boats
  10. Place pasta and chicken breast into oven
  11. Cook pasta for 12min then allow to cool for 10min
  12. Cook chicken breast for ~20min, or until 165F in center
