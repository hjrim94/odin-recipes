jamabalaya-rice

Description

A southern classic, jambalaya rice is easy to make if using store bought package and even easier to eat if using natural orifices

It can be made easily using a few pots and pans and results in a dish that is to die for

Ingredients

  • 57.6gm rice/day (jambalaya rice mix)
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons of butter
  • 226gm chicken breast/day
  • 44.8gm black beans/day
  • salt
  • pepper
  • olive oil

Steps

  1. bring 2 cups water to boil
  2. Preheat oven to 450F
  3. add butter and rice mix and bring to boil
  4. Boil for 30 seconds, then simmer for 25min
  5. Season chicken w/salt and pepper
  6. Bake in oven for 20min, or until internal temp is at least 165F
  7. Add beans to rice and mix
  8. Let sit
